About Glo Foods

Our journey from humble beginnings to culinary excellence

Our Story

At Glo Foods, we're passionate about bringing the vibrant flavors and relaxed atmosphere of a classic fish fry right to your plate. Rooted in the simple joy of fresh seafood cooked to perfection, we've created a space where quality ingredients and good times come together. Whether you're craving the satisfying crunch of perfectly fried fish or the smoky char of the grill, our commitment is to serve up delicious, unpretentious meals that remind you of sunny days and the taste of the ocean.

Our focus is on sourcing the freshest catches and preparing them with care. From flaky swordfish and hearty marlin to the light and flavorful mahi-mahi (dolphin), we take pride in the quality of our seafood. We believe in keeping things simple – letting the natural taste of the fish shine through, complemented by our crispy, golden chips and homemade sauces. At Glo Foods, it's not just about the food; it's about creating a welcoming experience where you can kick back, relax, and enjoy a fantastic meal with friends and family.

More than just a fish fry grill, Glo Foods is a part of the community. We strive to be a place where locals and visitors alike can gather for a taste of authentic, satisfying food in a laid-back setting. Our friendly service and dedication to quality are at the heart of everything we do. Come on down to Glo Foods and taste the difference that fresh ingredients and a love for good food make. We look forward to serving you!

Our Journey

Key milestones in the Glo Foods story

1986

The Beginning

Gloria Thompson establishes Glo Foods as a small family catering service operating from her home kitchen in Bath.

1992

First Kitchen Facility

Growing demand leads to opening our first dedicated kitchen space and hiring our initial team of professional chefs.

1997

Next Generation

Michael Thompson joins the family business after completing his culinary training at Le Cordon Bleu London.

2005

Expansion

Glo Foods moves to a purpose-built facility with larger kitchens and a dedicated event space for tastings and showcases.

2008

New Leadership

Michael Thompson takes over as CEO, bringing contemporary vision while maintaining the company's founding principles.

2015

Sustainability Focus

Launch of our sustainability initiative, committing to local sourcing, reducing waste, and eco-friendly practices.

2023

Today

Glo Foods continues to evolve, now serving over 200 weddings and 500 corporate events annually across the South West.
